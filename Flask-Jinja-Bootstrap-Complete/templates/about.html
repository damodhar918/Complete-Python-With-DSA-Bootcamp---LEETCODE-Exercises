About Flask Blog

Welcome to Our Community

Flask Blog is a modern platform for sharing ideas and connecting with readers worldwide.

Our Mission

We believe in the power of storytelling and the importance of having a voice. Flask Blog provides a simple, elegant platform for anyone to share their thoughts, expertise, and creativity with the world.

What We Offer

  • Easy Publishing: Write and publish posts in minutes
  • Community Engagement: Comment and discuss with other writers
  • User Profiles: Build your personal brand and following
  • Categories: Organize content by topic
  • Analytics: Track your posts' performance
  • Newsletter: Stay updated with the latest content

Technology Stack

Flask Blog is built with modern web technologies:

  • Backend: Flask & Python
  • Database: SQLAlchemy & SQLite
  • Authentication: Flask-Login
  • Forms: Flask-WTF
  • Frontend: Bootstrap 5
  • Templating: Jinja2
  • Icons: Font Awesome
  • API: RESTful JSON
